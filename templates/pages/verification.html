Verification Process

Authentic Proof of Execution

Codexx Academy employs a rigorous multi-stage verification process to ensure that every portfolio represents real work and a real professional.

01. Identity Verification

We confirm the identity of the portfolio owner. Anonymous profiles or fake entities are not permitted in our public gallery. This ensures direct accountability for the work shown.

02. Project Proof

Every project listed must be accompanied by proof of execution. This includes live links, source code access (where applicable), or client testimonials that we can independently verify.

03. Role & Ownership

We verify the specific role the member played in each project. Whether they were a solo builder, a team lead, or a specialist, their contribution must be accurately represented.

04. Ongoing Activity

Verification is not a one-time event. We monitor ongoing activity to ensure that the "Verified" status remains current and reflective of active execution.

Why This Matters

When you see the Verified Badge on a Codexx Academy portfolio, you are looking at a professional whose history has been vetted. We provide the infrastructure of trust so you can focus on the work.
