Academy Standards & Terms

Guidelines for Platform Access and Professional Verification

How to Reach Full Access

Full Access is granted to professionals who demonstrate consistent activity and technical readiness.

  • Complete at least 3 production-ready projects on your portfolio.
  • Ensure all project descriptions and assets are fully populated.
  • Maintain an active presence and respond to inquiries.
Mode: Restricted (Demo) is default for new accounts.

The Verified Badge (POW)

The "Verified Professional" badge is the highest trust indicator on Codexx Academy.

  • Automated Audits: AI systems monitor portfolio quality, code proof, and activity.
  • Visibility: Only "Verified" portfolios appear in the global gallery on the homepage.
  • Manual Review: Final vetting is performed by Academy auditors to ensure "Proof of Work" standards.
Terms of Professional Usage

All projects presented must be authentic "Proof of Work". Misrepresentation of skills or external projects as your own will lead to permanent removal.

Direct communication with clients must remain professional. The platform serves as a bridge; however, the Academy's reputation is tied to your conduct.
